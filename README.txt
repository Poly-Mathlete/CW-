2048 Game
Welcome to 2048! The classic sliding puzzle game where you combine matching numbers to reach the coveted 2048 tile. Are you ready to challenge your skills and achieve the highest score possible?

How to Play
Move the tiles: Use the arrow keys (or swipe on mobile) to move the tiles in the grid.
Combine matching tiles: When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one! For example, two "2" tiles will combine into a "4".
Goal: Keep combining tiles until you reach the 2048 tile! But the fun doesn’t stop there—try to keep playing for the highest score possible.
Game Over: The game ends when the grid is full, and no more moves are possible.
Features
Intuitive Controls: Arrow keys or swipe gestures (depending on platform).
Responsive Design: Play on any device, from your desktop to your mobile.
Leaderboard: Track your best scores and challenge your friends.
Simple Yet Addictive: One of the most addictive puzzle games of all time.
